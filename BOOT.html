IMAGE OF THE DAY

A nebula (plural: nebulae) is an immense cloud of dust, hydrogen, helium, and other ionized gases found in interstellar space.
Nebulae are often referred to as "stellar nurseries" because they are regions where new stars are born. These magnificent
clouds come in various forms and colors, some glowing brightly due to the radiation of nearby stars, while others are dark,
absorbing light from surrounding sources.
